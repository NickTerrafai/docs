---
title: "Job Status"
description: "When polling, the job may have one of the following statuses:

"
---

| Status     | Meaning                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| processing | The job is still being processed. Continue polling at your preferred interval.                                                                                                                             |
| completed  | The job has finished successfully. The response will include the extraction results.                                                                                                                       |
| failed     | The job could not be completed due to an error. The response may include error details.                                                                                                                    |
| expired    | The job data is no longer available. This occurs in either of the following cases:<br />• The job has been retrieved 3 times.<br />• 12 hours have passed without any polling since the job was submitted. |
